Volunteer Experiences

Sri Sri Kaiblyadham Chattogram

Duration: January 2008 - Present

Position: Volunteer

Activities:

  • Every Friday, provide prasadam at Kaiblyadham, offering blessings and support to the devotees.
  • On every full moon night, Kaiblyadham celebrates with grand festivals, including worship and cultural events.
  • The biggest celebration is Durga Puja, where actively participate in organizing and managing the festivities.
  • Distributing warm clothes to all the devotees during the winter season, ensuring their comfort and well-being.

Actively assist in organizing events, helping with setup, coordination, and ensuring smooth operations during activities. Engage with participants, provide support during events, and contribute to community outreach and welfare initiatives.
